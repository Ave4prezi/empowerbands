Leadership with purpose

Board of Directors

Meet the leaders guiding EmpowerBands Worldwide as we advance safety, accessibility, and faster emergency response through smart wearable technology.

The people behind the mission

Our Leadership

Our board provides strategic direction, operational leadership, financial oversight, and community advocacy.

Founder

Chief Executive Officer

April McDaniel

April McDaniel founded EmpowerBands Worldwide with a vision to improve emergency response through innovative wearable technology. She leads the organization’s strategic direction, partnerships, fundraising initiatives, and long-term growth while keeping its mission centered on protecting vulnerable individuals and supporting families.

Co-Founder

Director of Operations

Avery Johnson

Avery Johnson oversees daily operations, community outreach, strategic partnerships, and organizational development. He brings leadership, entrepreneurship, and a commitment to community service while helping build relationships with schools, healthcare providers, first responders, and nonprofit organizations.

Treasurer

Financial Oversight

Selina

Selina provides financial oversight for EmpowerBands Worldwide, including budgeting, fiscal accountability, and organizational compliance. She helps ensure that resources are managed responsibly while supporting long-term sustainability and mission-driven growth.

Our commitment

Guided by integrity, innovation, and compassion.

The EmpowerBands Worldwide Board of Directors is committed to responsible governance, transparency, thoughtful growth, and technology that improves safety and independence.

01

Integrity

We lead with honesty, accountability, and responsible decision-making.

02

Accessibility

We consider diverse abilities, needs, and real-world situations in our work.

03

Innovation

We pursue practical technology that helps people when every second matters.

04

Community

We collaborate with families, caregivers, schools, organizations, and public-safety partners.
